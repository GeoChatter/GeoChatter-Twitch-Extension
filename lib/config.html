How to find your GeoGuessr ID

  1. Go to your GeoGuessr profile page
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  3. Locate SHARE PROFILE tab
  4. Copy the ID at the end of the link
    • Example profile link:
      • https://www.geoguessr.com/user/5d700ceab4ec17ea0444e453
    • The part of the link to copy and paste from the link above:
      • 5d700ceab4ec17ea0444e453
